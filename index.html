A Taste That Stays With You Forever

Nestled in the heart of Loni, Ghaziabad, Red Flame Restaurant is more than just a place to eat — it's an experience. We started with a simple dream: to bring authentic, mouth-watering Indian flavors to every table.

Our chefs combine traditional recipes with modern techniques, ensuring every dish is a masterpiece. From sizzling tandoori grills to rich curries and fresh naans, every bite is a celebration.
